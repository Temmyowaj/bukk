Ewa Agonyin

Ewa Agonyin is an African made street food common in nigeria .the beans are made to be extremely soft or mashed.it is eaten commonly with barely ground pepper and tomota sauce which is very spicy,but peppery .additional ingredients can include palm oil,onion and crayfish.
